Track Your Chess Progress

Unlock the power of data with our tool! Create detailed reports of your chess games from chess.com and lichess.org for free. Discover your strengths and weaknesses, analyze your strategies, and improve your game.

Winning Insights

Get the winning edge! Check your win ratio per opening, per time control, and even per day of the week. Identify where you excel and where you need to focus your efforts to become a better chess player.

Game Analysis Made Easy

Experience the convenience of in-depth game analysis. Our tool breaks down your games into phases and highlights your mistakes, helping you learn and grow as a chess player.
